About Vivekananda Global University

Vivekananda Global University (VGU) is a private university located in Jaipur, Rajasthan, India. It offers various undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, management, law, and other disciplines.

VGU is committed to providing quality education, fostering innovation, and nurturing students to become responsible global citizens.

About Sunstone

Sunstone Eduversity is a leading edtech company offering industry-aligned management programs in partnership with universities and colleges. It emphasizes experiential learning, mentorship-driven programs, and career-oriented education.

Through its innovative approach, Sunstone aims to bridge the gap between academia and industry, preparing students for successful careers in the corporate world.
